## Help

This category holds onboarding guides for contributors joining the PointSav platform. It is organised by contributor profile: engineers who write code, writers who produce documentation and editorial content, and designers who work with the visual and component substrate.

Each guide walks a new contributor through the tooling, conventions, and commit flow they need to begin working. The guides are procedural in register — they describe how to do something, step by step, rather than explaining what a system is. For conceptual explanations, see the articles in [Architecture](/architecture/) and [Reference](/reference/). For style and genre conventions specific to documentation writing, see [Reference](/reference/).

The guides in this category reference the staging-tier commit flow, the cluster-clone development pattern, and the editorial pipeline that routes drafts from contributors to the published wiki. Readers do not need to understand the full system before starting; the guides introduce the relevant parts in the order they are needed.

Articles in this category are planned. The category will populate as the platform documentation expands.
