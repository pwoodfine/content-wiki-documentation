## Governance

This category covers the formal decision records, licensing posture, contributor model, and compliance conventions that govern how the PointSav platform is built, licensed, and changed over time. Governance articles are the written record of decisions that have been made and the rationale behind them; they are not aspirational statements.

Architecture decision records (ADRs) document specific design choices that carry system-wide consequences — each ADR states the decision, the context, the options considered, and the reasoning. Licensing articles explain the licence matrix that governs each repository and its contents. The contributor model article describes the three-tier structure through which code and content flow from contributors to the canonical platform. Compliance conventions document the posture required by applicable regulatory instruments, including continuous-disclosure obligations under Canadian securities law.
